#Chat

We have a friendly community over at the PostCSS Gitter channel which we recommend for general queries and plugin discussion.

#Twitter

Our cssnano twitter account will keep you up to date with any new developments. We will also share tweets on interesting web performance topics, usually relating to CSS compression. So why not follow us!
